Three days later...

You have adopted a puppy from the local shelter, and you are taking him out for a walk.

You pass the forest where you lost your old pal. You hear a twig snap.

A bush by the edge of the woods starts shaking, and you see your first dog jump out!

Your grin grows wider than the Grand Canyon as run to her to hug her close.

But she sees your new dog, and growls at you, feeling betrayed.

You never should have given up on looking for her!

Hope you learned your lesson. What a cruel heart you have.
